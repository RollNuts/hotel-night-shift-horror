## Intake Rules For New Asset Rows

Use these rules before any front-desk art-density asset enters source control:

- Project-authored assets may use `Project-authored` and `Project-owned`, with public-repo redistribution set to `Yes`.
- Verified CC0/public-domain-style assets from sources such as Poly Haven, ambientCG, and Kenney may be committed only after the specific asset page and license are checked and recorded in `Notes`.
- Other free assets may be committed only when the specific license explicitly allows commercial use, modification, attribution compliance, and public source redistribution of raw or converted files.
- Fab/Marketplace raw assets must use public-repo redistribution `No` unless a specific license or written permission explicitly allows public source redistribution; if redistribution is `No`, do not commit the raw asset file.
- If the source, creator rights, license version, attribution, or redistribution permission is unclear, do not import the asset.

## Allowed Use Values

- `internal-only`
- `public-repo`
- `playtest`
- `demo`
- `store`
- `trailer`
- `release`

If license status is unclear, do not import the asset.
